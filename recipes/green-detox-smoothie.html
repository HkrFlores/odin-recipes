Green Detox Smoothie

Description

Healthie Smoothie

After completed this recipe you can eat with someone or just enjoy by yourself

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 pear, chopped
  • 1/4 green apple, chopped
  • 1/4 avocado, chopped
  • 3 broccoli florets

Steps

  1. Blend pineapple juice, spinach, pear, apple, avocado, and broccoli florets together in a blender until smooth.
